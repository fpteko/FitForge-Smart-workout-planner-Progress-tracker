About FitForge

How it works and why it was built.

💡 What is FitForge?

FitForge is a smart workout planner and progress tracker built to help people remove the guesswork from fitness. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced lifter, FitForge generates personalized workout plans and tracks your real progress over time with beautiful charts.

🎯 The Problem

Many people want to work out but face common challenges:

  • Don't know how to structure workouts properly
  • Can't easily track progress session-to-session
  • Lose motivation without visual feedback

FitForge solves all three by generating workouts, tracking your input, and showing your progress visually.

⚙️ How It Works

1

Choose Your Goal

Select a fitness goal (strength, cardio, or weight loss) and your experience level on the Workout Builder page.

2

Generate a Workout

FitForge fetches exercises from the API Ninjas Exercise API based on your selected muscle group and difficulty.

3

Save & Track

Save workouts you like, then log your sets, reps, and weight on the Progress Tracker page.

4

Visualize Progress

Watch your charts update in real time — see total volume lifted, workout frequency, and your current streak.
